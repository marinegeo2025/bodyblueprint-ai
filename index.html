本来の面目
Honrai no Menmoku

Driven by science. Powered by AI. Built to optimize your aesthetic health.

本来の面目 "Honrai no Menmoku" is a Zen Buddhist phrase meaning "your original face"—the true self before the world shaped you.

We each begin this life in perfect wholeness—our original face, pure and unclouded by the world. Over time, distractions and doubts obscure our natural brilliance. Yet deep within, that radiant potential remains.

本来の面目 (Honrai no Menmoku) calls us back to our true selves. Guided by AI and shaped by science, this app is a companion on your path—helping you nourish body and mind in harmony. With the compassionate blessing of Kannon‐san, Bodhisattva of Mercy, you step forward. Empowered to uncover the shining truth you have always carried, you realize that the journey is simply returning home to the perfection that was there from the start.

To use Honrai No Menmoku

1) Start by calculating your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) on the BMR page—this sets your daily calorie target based on your fitness goal (cut, bulk, or maintain).

2) Track your daily weight on the Weight page and record your meals on the Meals page to get AI-powered nutrition analysis.

3) For personalized fitness and nutrition advice, visit the AI Chat page and ask Al, your AI fitness trainer, specific questions.

Each page of this app is designed to simplify your pathway to aesthetic health with actionable insights.

Bodhisattva Kannonsan (観音菩薩), the Goddess of Mercy embodies boundless compassion and is said to guide all beings toward liberation.

Data Security & Privacy Statement

The developer of 本来の面目 Honrai no Menmoku, Alex Barnard Ph.D., is committed to fostering trust by protecting your data and being transparent about how it’s used. This app stores all user data locally on your device. No data is transferred to, or shared with, any other devices or external services. Because the app is standalone, all locally stored information remains on your device.

Please note: Some of the data you enter is sent to OpenAI ’s API for processing. For details on how OpenAI handles security and privacy, visit their Security & Privacy pages.
